01 — Core Vision
The market provided ownership provenance. A(DAI) supplies provenance of meaning—the missing semantic layer. Not a platform, not a marketplace. A persistent, contestable graph where practitioners and artworks accumulate relational mass through density and diversity, not fame or market value.
Coherence without consensus. Two contradictory readings coexist when both are traceable, neither deprecated, and the tension itself becomes a named entity. Disagreement is evidence.

03 — Strategy
Mode 1 — Commons
Open, field-owned intelligence under CC-BY-SA. Never sold. The actual work. Scaffolding, not delegation—practitioner agents surface candidates; practitioners review and submit.
Mode 2 — Advisory
Decision-support services for institutional actors (collectors, curators) built atop Mode 1 without replacing it. Revenue enables the commons.

06 — Tidal Sensing Loop

Tidal rhythm—not continuous, on-demand, or real-time. Three nested cycles detect different scales of signal, plus external calibration and rapid-response interrupts.

6h
The Pulse
Lightweight metric scan. Flags orphans, imbalances, stale nodes, interpretive diversity gaps. Output: intention gap report.
24h
The Survey
Topological analysis—clusters, bridges, structural holes. Artwork clustering and contribution patterns. Output: intention observations.
72h
The Dream
Consolidates lived experience, tendency vocabulary, counter-signals. Field-level question formation. Output: generative prompts.
External Calibration
Quarterly blind spot audits against festival submissions, platform data, institutional records.
Rapid-Response Interrupts
Category-level disruptions escalate in hours. Automated post-Basel.
07 — Architecture
Current state: single DB. Basel MVP runs one adai.db on Fly.io Frankfurt. Schema is migration-ready for the split—node IDs map deterministically to DB files. ATTACH DATABASE planned for cross-DB queries (limit ~10 per query).
Planned Layer 1
Practitioner DB
1 file per practitioner. All signals, edges, metadata, full provenance. Portable—"give me my data" = hand them a .db file.
Planned Layer 2
Scene DB
1 per scene (e.g. "Berlin electronic music"). Index of practitioners, scene-level signals, materialized aggregates. References, not copies.
Live Layer 3
Field DB
Currently the only layer. Single adai.db with CR-SQLite CRDT extension. 774 nodes, 929 edges, deployed on Fly.io fra region.
Ouroboros defense. Cycle detection against system-generated feedback loops. Source-origin typing from human_primary through ai_generated. Raw practitioner voice is highest-value signal.
